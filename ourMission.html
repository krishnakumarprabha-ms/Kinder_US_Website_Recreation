THE KINDER MISSION

At the heart of the KINDER™ brand is the commitment to sparking, sharing and celebrating the simple joys of childhood. After all, it’s the simple joys children experience that make childhood magical. That’s why, for almost 50 years, we have been delighting families all over the world through innovative products and experiences specially created to help parents and children share simple moments of joy together. So go on and surprise your loved ones with a KINDER JOY™!
